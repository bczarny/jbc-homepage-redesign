Executive speaking & awards

Amplifying the founder's voice.

Pulling focus on stages beyond the screen is a key pillar of how we elevate founder-led brands. The JBC Executive Speaking team develops original thought leadership narratives and talking points to be activated across conferences, keynotes, and roundtable discussions.

We spearhead awards submissions to present our brand partners with industry recognition — from consumer lists to category honors to business-of-the-year accolades that compound over time.

How we work

Four stages. No dead air.

Every engagement runs on the same cadence — set the strategy, build the runway, activate against the news cycle, and keep compounding. Your dedicated team operates as an extension of your in-house org from week one.

01

Immerse

Deep dive on brand, product, category, and audience. We leave the kickoff with a full messaging matrix and a ranked list of the reporters and outlets that move the needle for your specific goal.

Weeks 1 — 2
02

Architect

Strategy build — integrated plan across Media Relations, Affiliate, Influencer, and Content. Proof points, KPIs, and the shared scorecard go live before any pitch leaves the building.

Weeks 2 — 4
03

Activate

Ongoing press office — proactive pitching, inbound management, spokesperson prep, and cultural newsjacking. We move in hours, not weeks, when the news cycle rewards speed.

Months 2+
04

Compound

Weekly reporting, quarterly strategy re-cuts, executive positioning, and awards. Each win is re-cut into owned and paid — placements stop being one-offs and start being cumulative.

Ongoing
