An agency of humans

JBC is an agency of humans. We work in media relations because we're people's people — and we love to amplify our team as they achieve their goals, both professionally and personally.

We strive to make our workplace one where team members are not only supported across their short- and long-term trajectory of career growth, but are also fulfilled as people, in and out of the office.

Life at JBC

Here's what life looks like at JBC.

Four pillars we hold ourselves to — for the team, not just the client roster.

01 · Schedule

Compassionate scheduling.

Your work is not your life, and it shouldn't be. Flexible schedules, hybrid return-to-office plans, an open paid time off policy, a four-day workweek through the summer, additional paid caregiver leave, and more.

02 · Benefits

Comprehensive benefits.

Robust health insurance for all employees, a 401(k) matching plan to invest in the future, monthly cell-phone reimbursement, and an open leave policy that includes pregnancy and fertility care.

03 · Mental health

Mental health support.

Brains as well as bodies. Agency-wide monthly Mental Health Days, plus additional mental-health paid time away for parents and caregivers every week of the year.

04 · Culture

Immersive culture.

In-house committees plan monthly programming — internships, team-building, employee recognition, philanthropic opportunities throughout the year. (Ask us about our Culture Committee.)

We're people's people — and the team reflects it.
