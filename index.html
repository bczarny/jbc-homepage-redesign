How we measure

Real, trackable PR. Proprietary to you.

Results mean nothing if they can't be quantified. That's why data informs every decision we make — and every recommendation we deliver.

01

Strategic Narrative

The story we're telling, and whether it's landing. We track message pull-through, share of voice against category competitors, and the narrative ground we own versus the ground still worth taking.

Pull-through · share of voice · narrative ownership
02

Brand Health & Quality

The texture and tier of coverage, not just the volume. We monitor tone, sentiment, outlet quality, and the mix of tier-one editorial versus contextual mentions — so the brand's perception is compounding, not just its footprint.

Sentiment · outlet tier · quality of coverage
03

Business Outcomes & Attribution

What the work actually moves. We tie earned coverage back to the metrics that matter — web traffic, affiliate revenue, fundraise momentum, retail conversations — and report the through-line every month.

Attributable revenue · traffic · pipeline impact
