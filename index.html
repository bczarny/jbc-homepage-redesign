What we do

A holistic approach, told quickly and with intention.

Founded as the antidote to traditional PR, our senior bench of publicists and specialized strategists shares a common theme: we have the best stories to tell — and we tell them fast.

JBC serves as a strategic communications partner to leading organizations across the full range of consumer sectors. We build programs that create true consumer affinity and thought leadership — not one-off placements, but a consistent drumbeat of coverage that compounds into category leadership.

Every engagement starts with the brand's objectives and works backward: the audiences that matter, the narratives that move them, and the outlets, stages, and moments where those narratives land hardest.
