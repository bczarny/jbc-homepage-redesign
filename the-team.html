The JBC bench · NY & LA

A senior bench. One standard.

The bench

JBC is led by Jennifer Bett Meyer and Melissa Duren Conner and built out of expert publicists, category specialists, content leads, affiliate operators, and executive-speaking strategists — working shoulder-to-shoulder across New York and Los Angeles.

Every account is staffed by senior practitioners who have done the work before. Founders don't get routed through a junior relay; the person pitching your story is the person who understands why it matters.

Leadership

Founder-led. Still.

Founded in 2014 and still personally run by its founders — which means the people who set the agency's standard are the people accountable to the work every day.

President & Founder

Jennifer Bett Meyershe / her

For over 18 years, Jennifer has led the communications strategy for category-defining consumer brands. A graduate of Wesleyan University, she began her career as an editor at Harper's Bazaar and Marie Claire before launching JBC in January 2014 to serve a founder-led roster across beauty, fashion, food, wellness, and lifestyle.

Partner & Managing Director

Melissa Duren Connershe / her

A publicist and strategic thinker with nearly 15 years of industry experience, Melissa joined JBC in July 2014 as the agency's Partner. Her bylines and thought leadership have appeared in Glossy, AdWeek, and PR News; PRSA named her to its inaugural Rising Leaders class in 2017.

EVP & Head of Growth

Libbey Baumgartenshe / her

Libbey joined JBC in 2015 to build out its Impact, Health & Wellness division. She now leads the agency's growth function — evaluating inbound brands against JBC's values-first filter and architecting the programs that win new partnerships.

EVP, Head of Strategy

Ilana Rubin Dvirshe / her

As Head of Strategy, Ilana shapes how JBC translates brand priorities into media outcomes. She partners with founders and executives across the roster to define narrative architecture, category positioning, and the newsjacking frameworks that keep clients ahead of the cycle.

How we staff

Senior on every account.

Four structural commitments that define how we show up — the reason the same senior lead who won the pitch is still the senior lead on the Slack thread two years later.

01

Founder-led, still

Founded in 2014 and never sold. The partners who started the agency are still running it — accountable to the work and to every client relationship.

02

Senior on every account

The person who won your pitch is the person on the Slack thread two years later. No junior handoffs, no bait-and-switch — the relationship stays with the lead who built it.

03

A DEI lens

Everything we do is reviewed through a DEI lens, backed by ongoing monthly educational sessions and strategic internal hires. Not a chapter — a filter.

04

No outsourced divisions

Affiliate, Influencer, Content, and Executive Speaking all live in-house. One team, one P&L, one scorecard — no middlemen between the strategy and the execution.
